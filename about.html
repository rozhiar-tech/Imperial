Art Museum, Poland
Art Museum is an international project located in Poland. It has various different levels, whom are embedded into the unevenness of the terrain. This project seeks to integrate passive strategies for energy saving, such as the inclusion of as much natural light as possible as well as having green roofs with lots of vegetation in them. The geometry of this house combines design with nature into the structure of the house, which makes it unique amongst other neighboring places.
